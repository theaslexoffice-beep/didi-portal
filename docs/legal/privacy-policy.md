## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Our Commitment
DIDI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our grievance redressal platform.

### 1.2 Legal Compliance
This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:
- **Information Technology Act, 2000** (IT Act)
- **Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011**
- **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023** (DPDP Act)
- **Constitution of India** (Article 21 - Right to Privacy)

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We collect the following personal information:

| Data Element | Mandatory | Purpose |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Full Name | Yes | Identity verification, communication |
| WhatsApp Number | Yes | Primary communication channel, OTP verification |
| Email Address | No | Alternative communication, updates |
| Residential Address | Yes (location-specific) | Grievance validation, authority jurisdiction |
| Government ID (optional) | No | Enhanced verification when required |

### 2.2 Complaint Data
- **Complaint text and descriptions**
- **Media uploads** (photographs, videos, documents)
- **Location data** (GPS coordinates when provided)
- **Timestamps** of submission and updates
- **Category and tags** assigned to complaints

### 2.3 Technical Data
- **IP address** (anonymized where possible)
- **Device information** (browser type, operating system)
- **Access logs** (timestamps of platform usage)
- **Cookies** (essential for platform functionality)

### 2.4 Communication Data
- Messages exchanged with authorities
- Follow-up correspondence
- Resolution updates and status changes

---

## 3. Purpose of Data Collection

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
We use your data exclusively for:
1. **Grievance Processing**: To receive, review, and forward your complaints to relevant authorities
2. **Identity Verification**: To ensure authenticity of complaints and prevent misuse
3. **Communication**: To contact you regarding your complaint status, updates, or clarifications
4. **Authority Coordination**: To facilitate communication between you and government authorities

### 3.2 Secondary Purposes
With appropriate safeguards:
1. **Platform Improvement**: Analyzing complaint patterns to improve our services
2. **Research & Advocacy**: Generating anonymized reports on civic issues (no personal identifiers)
3. **Legal Compliance**: Fulfilling obligations under applicable laws

### 3.3 What We NEVER Do
We explicitly **DO NOT**:
- ❌ Sell your personal data to any third party
- ❌ Use your data for commercial advertising
- ❌ Share your data for marketing purposes
- ❌ Transfer data outside India without legal basis
- ❌ Use automated decision-making that affects your rights

---

## 4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

### 4.1 Sharing with Authorities
Your data may be shared with:
- **Relevant Government Departments** (Municipal Corporation, District Administration, etc.)
- **Public Sector Undertakings** (when grievance relates to them)
- **Law Enforcement** (when required by law or court order)

**Scope of Sharing:**
- Only data necessary for grievance resolution
- Only with authorities having jurisdiction over the matter
- Through secure, documented channels

### 4.2 Legal Disclosures
We may disclose data when:
- Required by court order or legal process
- Necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety
- Required under the IT Act, DPDP Act, or other applicable laws
- To prevent fraud, abuse, or illegal activities

### 4.3 Service Providers
We may engage third-party service providers for:
- **Cloud hosting** (data stored within India)
- **WhatsApp Business API** (for messaging)
- **Analytics** (anonymized data only)

All service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and data protection obligations.

### 4.4 No Unauthorized Sharing
Except as stated above, your data is **never** shared with:
- Political parties or candidates
- Private companies for commercial purposes
- Media outlets (without explicit consent)
- International organizations (without legal basis)

---

## 5. Data Security Measures

### 5.1 Technical Safeguards
We implement the following security measures:
- **Encryption**: 
  - AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  - TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  - End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Firewalls**: Next-generation firewall protection
- **Regular Security Audits**: Quarterly penetration testing
- **Backup Systems**: Encrypted, geographically distributed backups

### 5.2 Organizational Measures
- **Privacy by Design**: Data protection integrated into platform architecture
- **Staff Training**: Regular privacy and security awareness programs
- **Incident Response**: Documented breach response procedures
- **Vendor Management**: Due diligence on all service providers

### 5.3 Compliance with IT Rules
Our security practices comply with:
- **ISO/IEC 27001** standards (or equivalent)
- **IT Act, 2000** security requirements
- **DPDP Act, 2023** data protection obligations

### 5.4 Breach Notification
In case of a data breach:
- Affected users will be notified within **72 hours** of discovery
- Notification to **Data Protection Board of India** as required under DPDP Act
- Transparent communication about the nature and impact of the breach

---

## 6. User Rights

Under the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023** and other applicable laws, you have the following rights:

### 6.1 Right to Access
You can request:
- A copy of your personal data
- Information about how your data is processed
- Details of data sharing with third parties

**Process:** Email request to privacy@didi.org.in with identity verification

### 6.2 Right to Correction
You can request correction of:
- Inaccurate personal information
- Outdated contact details
- Incorrect grievance details

**Timeline:** Corrections within **7 working days**

### 6.3 Right to Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)
You can request deletion of your data when:
- The data is no longer necessary for the purpose
- You withdraw consent (where applicable)
- Data was processed unlawfully

**Exceptions:** We may retain data where required by law or for legal claims.

### 6.4 Right to Grievance Redressal
If you have concerns about our data practices:
1. Contact our Data Protection Officer
2. File complaint with **Data Protection Board of India**
3. Approach appropriate courts under DPDP Act

### 6.5 Right to Nominate
You can nominate another individual to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity.

## 7. Data Retention

### 7.1 Retention Periods

| Data Category | Retention Period | Rationale |
|--------------|------------------|-----------|
| Active complaints | Duration of resolution + 1 year | Follow-up and appeals |
| Resolved complaints | 3 years from resolution | Legal claims, audit |
| User account data | 1 year after account deletion | Legal obligations |
| Communication logs | 2 years | Dispute resolution |
| Media uploads | Same as complaint | Evidence preservation |
| Server logs | 6 months | Security monitoring |

### 7.2 Early Deletion
You may request early deletion, subject to:
- Legal retention requirements
- Ongoing legal proceedings
- Public interest considerations

### 7.3 Secure Disposal
Data is securely deleted using:
- Cryptographic erasure for encrypted data
- Secure overwrite for storage media
- Physical destruction where applicable

## 3. डेटा संग्रह का उद्देश्य

### 3.1 प्राथमिक उद्देश्य
हम आपके डेटा का उपयोग विशेष रूप से इसके लिए करते हैं:
1. **शिकायत प्रसंस्करण**: आपकी शिकायतें प्राप्त, समीक्षा और संबंधित अधिकारियों को अग्रेषित करने के लिए
2. **पहचान सत्यापन**: शिकायतों की प्रामाणिकता सुनिश्चित करने और दुरुपयोग रोकने के लिए
3. **संचार**: आपकी शिकायत स्थिति, अपडेट या स्पष्टीकरण के बारे में आपसे संपर्क करने के लिए
4. **अधिकारी समन्वय**: आप और सरकारी अधिकारियों के बीच संचार की सुविधा के लिए

### 3.2 हम कभी नहीं करते
हम स्पष्ट रूप से **नहीं** करते:
- ❌ आपका व्यक्तिगत डेटा किसी तीसरे पक्ष को बेचना
- ❌ आपके डेटा का उपयोग व्यावसायिक विज्ञापन के लिए करना
- ❌ डेटा विपणन उद्देश्यों के लिए साझा करना
- ❓ बिना कानूनी आधार के भारत के बाहर डेटा स्थानांतरित करना

## 4. डेटा साझाकरण और प्रकटीकरण

### 4.1 अधिकारियों के साथ साझाकरण
आपका डेटा इसके साथ साझा किया जा सकता है:
- **संबंधित सरकारी विभाग** (नगर निगम, जिला प्रशासन, आदि)

### 4.2 अनधिकृत साझाकरण नहीं

- राजनीतिक दल या उम्मीदवार
- व्यावसायिक उद्देश्यों के लिए निजी कंपनियां
- मीडिया आउटलेट (स्पष्ट सहमति के बिना)

  - आराम पर डेटा के लिए AES-256 एन्क्रिप्शन

### 6.2 सुधार का अधिकार
आप इसके सुधार का अनुरोध कर सकते हैं:
- गलत व्यक्तिगत जानकारी
- पुराना संपर्क विवरण
- गलत शिकायत विवरण

- डेटा उद्देश्य के लिए अब आवश्यक नहीं है
- आप सहमति वापस लेते हैं (जहां लागू हो)

### 6.4 शिकायत निवारण का अधिकार
यदि आपको हमारी डेटा प्रथाओं के बारे में चिंताएं हैं:
1. हमारे डेटा संरक्षण अधिकारी से संपर्क करें
2. **भारत के डेटा संरक्षण बोर्ड** के साथ शिकायत दर्ज करें
3. DPDP अधिनियम के तहत उचित अदालतों का दृष्टिकोण अपनाएं
