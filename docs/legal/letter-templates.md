# Authority Communication Templates

**DIDI - Digital Indian Democracy Initiative**  
*"Apni Samasya Hamare Saath Baatein"*  
**Version:** 1.0

---

This document provides standardized templates for communicating citizen grievances to government authorities. All templates are provided in both **English** and **Hindi**.

---

## Template 1: First Notification to Authority

### English Version

```
[DIDI Letterhead]

Ref. No.: DIDI/[COMPLAINT_ID]/[YYYY]
Date: [DATE]

To,
[AUTHORITY_NAME]
[DESIGNATION]
[DEPARTMENT]
[ADDRESS]
[CITY], [STATE] - [PIN]

Subject: Citizen Grievance - [BRIEF_DESCRIPTION] - Ref: [COMPLAINT_ID]

Respected Sir/Madam,

This is to bring to your kind attention a public grievance submitted by a citizen through the DIDI (Digital Indian Democracy Initiative) platform - a not-for-profit citizen grievance redressal system operating in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

**Complaint Details:**

- **Complaint ID:** [COMPLAINT_ID]
- **Date of Submission:** [SUBMISSION_DATE]
- **Complainant Name:** [CITIZEN_NAME] / Anonymous (Verified)
- **Contact Details:** [WHATSAPP_NUMBER] / [EMAIL]
- **Location/Area:** [LOCATION]
- **Category:** [CATEGORY] (e.g., Infrastructure, Sanitation, Water Supply, Road Maintenance, etc.)

**Nature of Grievance:**

[ISSUE_DESCRIPTION]

[Include detailed description of the problem, its impact on citizens, duration of the issue, and any previous attempts at resolution]

**Supporting Evidence:**

Attached herewith:
- [List of photographs/documents/videos]
- [Location coordinates, if applicable]
- [Any other relevant documentation]

**Citizen's Request:**

The complainant respectfully requests your intervention to:
[SPECIFIC_ACTION_REQUESTED]

**Legal Context (if applicable):**

[This grievance relates to the fundamental right to life and dignity under Article 21 of the Constitution of India / falls under the jurisdiction of [relevant law/regulation] / pertains to statutory obligations under [Act/Rules].]

**DIDI's Role:**

DIDI is a facilitation platform and does not represent the complainant in any legal capacity. We serve as a communication bridge between citizens and authorities to ensure grievances reach the appropriate departments efficiently.

We request your office to:
1. Acknowledge receipt of this complaint
2. Investigate the matter at the earliest
3. Communicate the action taken or proposed timeline to the complainant and DIDI
4. Update us on the resolution status for our records

**Response Requested:**

We would appreciate a response within **30 days** of receipt of this communication. The citizen and DIDI platform will be monitoring the progress of this grievance.

Should you require any clarification or additional information, please feel free to contact us at:

- Email: support@didi.org.in
- Phone: [PHONE_NUMBER]
- Website: [WEBSITE]

We trust in your commitment to public service and prompt action on this matter.

Thanking you,

Yours faithfully,

[SIGNATURE]
[NAME]
[DESIGNATION]
DIDI - Digital Indian Democracy Initiative
Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh

**Enclosures:**
1. Complaint details and supporting documents
2. Citizen verification details (on file)
3. Location map/photographs (if any)

**CC:**
- Complainant ([CITIZEN_NAME])
- [Relevant higher authority, if applicable]
- DIDI Platform Records
```

## Template 2: Follow-up Reminder (30-Day)

### English Version

```
[DIDI Letterhead]

Ref. No.: DIDI/[COMPLAINT_ID]/REMINDER-1/[YYYY]
Date: [DATE]

To,
[AUTHORITY_NAME]
[DESIGNATION]
[DEPARTMENT]
[ADDRESS]
[CITY], [STATE] - [PIN]

Subject: First Reminder - Citizen Grievance Ref: [COMPLAINT_ID] - [BRIEF_DESCRIPTION]

Respected Sir/Madam,

This is with reference to our letter dated **[FIRST_LETTER_DATE]** (Ref: DIDI/[COMPLAINT_ID]/[YYYY]) regarding a citizen grievance related to **[ISSUE_DESCRIPTION]**.

**Current Status:**

We regret to note that despite the passage of **30 days** since the initial communication, we have not received:
- Acknowledgment of the complaint
- Status update on investigation or action taken
- Expected timeline for resolution

**Impact on Citizen:**

The delay in addressing this grievance continues to cause:
- [Describe ongoing hardship/inconvenience]
- [Public safety concern, if applicable]
- [Any escalation of the problem since first report]

**Citizen Expectation:**

The complainant, [CITIZEN_NAME] / Anonymous (verified), remains hopeful of a positive response from your esteemed office and requests expedited action on this matter.

**Request for Urgent Action:**

We once again respectfully urge your office to:
1. **Acknowledge** this communication within 7 days
2. **Investigate** the matter on priority
3. **Provide a status update** to the complainant and DIDI
4. **Initiate corrective action** with a clear timeline

**Administrative Hierarchy:**

Should we not receive a substantive response within the next **15 days** (by [DEADLINE_DATE]), we may be constrained to:
- Escalate this matter to your supervising authority
- Invoke provisions of the Right to Information Act, 2005
- Seek citizen's grievance redressal through higher forums
- Highlight unresolved grievances through social media and public advocacy (in public interest)

**Contact Information:**

For any clarification or to provide updates, please reach us at:
- Email: support@didi.org.in
- Phone: [PHONE_NUMBER]
- Complaint Portal: [WEBSITE/COMPLAINT_TRACKING_LINK]

We trust that your office will treat this matter with the urgency it deserves and provide a satisfactory resolution at the earliest.

Thanking you,

Yours faithfully,

[SIGNATURE]
[NAME]
[DESIGNATION]
DIDI - Digital Indian Democracy Initiative
Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh

**CC:**
- [Supervising Authority/Higher Official]
- Complainant ([CITIZEN_NAME])
- DIDI Platform Records

**Note:** Copy of original complaint and this reminder are available at [COMPLAINT_TRACKING_URL]
```

## Template 3: Final Notice Before Escalation

### English Version

```
[DIDI Letterhead]

Ref. No.: DIDI/[COMPLAINT_ID]/FINAL-NOTICE/[YYYY]
Date: [DATE]

To,
[AUTHORITY_NAME]
[DESIGNATION]
[DEPARTMENT]
[ADDRESS]
[CITY], [STATE] - [PIN]

Subject: **FINAL NOTICE** - Unresolved Citizen Grievance - Ref: [COMPLAINT_ID]

Respected Sir/Madam,

This is our **FINAL NOTICE** regarding the unresolved citizen grievance (Ref: [COMPLAINT_ID]) initially communicated on **[FIRST_LETTER_DATE]** and reminded on **[REMINDER_DATE]**.

**Timeline Summary:**

- **Initial Complaint:** [FIRST_LETTER_DATE] (Day 0)
- **First Reminder:** [REMINDER_DATE] (Day 30)
- **Current Status:** Day [DAYS_ELAPSED] - **NO RESPONSE RECEIVED**

**Issue Description:**

**[ISSUE_DESCRIPTION]** - affecting [CITIZEN_NAME / community members] at [LOCATION].

**Seriousness of Non-Response:**

The continued inaction on this matter:
1. Violates the citizen's right to effective governance
2. Undermines public trust in government institutions
3. Perpetuates the hardship described in our earlier communications
4. [Any legal/safety implications]

**Good Governance Principles:**

As you are aware, principles of good governance and administrative accountability require timely response to citizen grievances. Non-responsiveness may attract:
- **Administrative scrutiny** under service delivery rules
- **RTI inquiries** seeking reasons for delay
- **Public accountability** measures
- **Legal recourse** available to citizens under applicable laws

**Escalation Plan (Effective [ESCALATION_DATE]):**

In the absence of a substantive response within **7 days** of this notice, DIDI will proceed with the following actions on behalf of the aggrieved citizen:

1. **Administrative Escalation:**
   - Forward complaint to [Higher Authority/Supervising Officer]
   - Notify District Collector/Commissioner
   - File complaint with [Relevant Ombudsman/Lokayukta]

2. **RTI Application:**
   - File RTI seeking reasons for non-action
   - Request details of officials responsible for this jurisdiction
   - Seek copies of any internal communications/reports on this matter

3. **Public Advocacy:**
   - Publish anonymized case study on DIDI platform (as unresolved grievance)
   - Share status on social media channels (Twitter, Facebook) with appropriate hashtags
   - Notify media outlets covering civic issues
   - Include in monthly "Unresponsive Authorities" report

4. **Legal Remedies:**
   - Advise citizen of legal options including:
     - Writ petition (if fundamental rights affected)
     - Consumer forum (if service deficiency)
     - Civil suit for damages (if applicable)

**Final Opportunity:**

We believe in the power of dialogue and administrative remedies. This notice provides your office a final opportunity to:
- **Acknowledge** the complaint
- **Provide reasons** for delay, if any
- **Commit to a resolution timeline**
- **Initiate corrective action** immediately

**Response Required By:** [DEADLINE_DATE] (7 days from date of this notice)

**Contact Information:**

- Email: support@didi.org.in (for fastest response)
- Phone: [PHONE_NUMBER]
- WhatsApp: [WHATSAPP_NUMBER]

We sincerely hope this matter can be resolved through mutual cooperation and prompt administrative action, avoiding the need for escalation.

Yours faithfully,

[SIGNATURE]
[NAME]
[DESIGNATION]
DIDI - Digital Indian Democracy Initiative
Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh

**CC:**
- [District Collector / Municipal Commissioner]
- [Supervising Authority]
- [Local Elected Representative, if applicable]
- Complainant ([CITIZEN_NAME])
- DIDI Legal & Advocacy Team

**Enclosure:** Complete complaint history and correspondence record
```
